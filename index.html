Computational Congressional Redistricting

UCSD CSE · Honors Thesis

An end-to-end geospatial ML pipeline that generates and scores U.S. redistricting plans over Census-2020 block data (158K–5.5M blocks per state). Three generators — a greedy DP + Kernighan–Lin heuristic, a Sequential Monte Carlo sampler, and XGBoost + multilevel graph partitioning — with VRA Section-2, Polsby–Popper, and efficiency-gap fairness metrics.
